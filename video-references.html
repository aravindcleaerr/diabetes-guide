🩸 How to Use a Glucometer — Video Guides

If you are new to blood glucose monitoring, these video guides walk you through the process step by step:

  • Accu-Chek: Search "Accu-Chek Instant how to use" — official video showing setup, test strip insertion, blood sample, and reading
  • OneTouch: Search "OneTouch Select Plus how to use" — step-by-step guide for one of India's most popular meters
  • FreeStyle Libre: Search "FreeStyle Libre sensor application" — how to apply the CGM sensor and scan for readings
Watch the video once, then watch it again while doing the test yourself. It becomes second nature after 2–3 times.

💉 Insulin Injection Technique Videos

  • BD Diabetes Care: "How to inject insulin with a pen" — proper technique, site rotation, needle disposal
  • Novo Nordisk: "FlexPen / FlexTouch injection guide" — specific to Novo Nordisk pens (Tresiba, NovoRapid, NovoMix)
  • Sanofi: "SoloStar pen injection technique" — for Lantus, Toujeo, and Apidra
  • Key points covered: Priming the pen, selecting the dose, choosing injection site, pinching skin, 90-degree angle, counting to 10 before withdrawing, site rotation
Improper injection technique (not rotating sites, injecting into lumps/lipodystrophy) is a common cause of unexplained glucose variability. Watch these videos even if you have been injecting for years.

🏋️ Exercise Videos for Diabetics

  • Yoga for Diabetes: Search "yoga for diabetes" — many channels offer 20–30 minute routines focusing on poses that stimulate the pancreas and improve insulin sensitivity (Surya Namaskar, Paschimottanasana, Ardha Matsyendrasana)
  • Walking workouts: Search "indoor walking workout" (Leslie Sansone / Walk at Home) — ideal for days when going outside is not possible
  • Chair exercises: Search "seated exercises for seniors with diabetes" — for those with mobility limitations or neuropathy
  • Resistance training: Search "resistance band exercises for beginners" — strength training improves insulin sensitivity and is recommended 2–3 times per week

🍳 Cooking Demos

  • Diabetes-friendly Indian cooking: Search "diabetic recipes Indian" — channels like Tarla Dalal, Nisha Madhulika, and Hebbars Kitchen have dedicated diabetes-friendly recipe collections
  • Low-carb Indian recipes: Roti alternatives (almond flour, coconut flour), cauliflower rice, millet-based dishes
  • ADA Diabetes Food Hub: Video recipes with nutritional information and carb counts
