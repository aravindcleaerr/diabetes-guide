Section 80DDB â€” Deduction for Medical Treatment of Specified Diseases:

Under the Income Tax Act, expenses incurred for the treatment of specified diseases (which can include severe diabetes complications) are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80DDB.

  • For individuals below 60 years: Deduction up to Rs 40,000 per year
  • For senior citizens (60+): Deduction up to Rs 1,00,000 per year
  • Requirement: A certificate from a specialist doctor (Form 10-I) confirming the diagnosis
  • Applicable for: Self, spouse, children, parents, and dependent siblings
Section 80D â€” Health Insurance Premium:

Premiums paid for health insurance (mediclaim) policies are deductible:

  • Up to Rs 25,000 for self and family (Rs 50,000 if senior citizen)
  • Additional Rs 25,000 for parents' health insurance (Rs 50,000 if parents are senior citizens)
  • Preventive health check-ups up to Rs 5,000 are included within this limit
