📚 Recommended Books

  • "Think Like a Pancreas" by Gary Scheiner — Practical guide to insulin management for Type 1 and insulin-using Type 2; considered the gold standard for insulin users
  • "Dr. Bernstein's Diabetes Solution" by Richard K. Bernstein — Low-carbohydrate approach to diabetes management by a physician who has lived with Type 1 for 70+ years
  • "Bright Spots & Landmines" by Adam Brown — Actionable diabetes tips based on personal experience and research; free PDF available from diaTribe
  • "The Diabetes Code" by Dr. Jason Fung — Explores the role of insulin resistance and dietary interventions in Type 2 diabetes
  • "Sugar Surfing" by Dr. Stephen Ponder — Dynamic glucose management using real-time CGM data
  • "Diabetes Burnout" by William Polonsky — Addressing the emotional and psychological burden of diabetes
  • "Using Insulin" by John Walsh — Comprehensive guide to insulin therapy, pump settings, and dose calculations
  • "Type 1 Diabetes in Children, Adolescents and Young Adults" by Dr. Ragnar Hanas — Essential reference for families managing Type 1 in young people
  • "Indian Diabetes Cookbook" by various authors — Search for cookbooks focused on diabetes-friendly Indian cuisine with regional recipes

📄 Clinical Guidelines (For Reference)

  • ADA Standards of Care in Diabetes (updated annually): The most comprehensive clinical guidelines; freely accessible at diabetesjournals.org
  • RSSDI Clinical Practice Recommendations: India-specific guidelines adapted for South Asian populations
  • ICMR Guidelines for Management of Type 2 Diabetes: Indian government-issued evidence-based guidelines
  • NICE Guidelines (UK): National Institute for Health and Care Excellence diabetes guidelines — well-structured and freely available
  • IDF Clinical Practice Recommendations: Global perspective on diabetes management
Clinical guidelines are written for healthcare professionals but are increasingly accessible to informed patients. Reading them can help you understand the reasoning behind your doctor's recommendations.
